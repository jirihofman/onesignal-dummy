How To Add Push Notifications Into Your Website

When you first visit this page, you will be prompted to allow or block notifications. If you allow, you will be added to the notification list and will receive a welcome notification.

After you allow notifications, you can send a notification from eg Appmixer flow or directly from the OneSignal dashboard.

Important: keep only one tab with this website open, close developer tools or bypass network
